How to Submit Your Video to LimeShelf

To upload your content to LimeShelf, follow these requirements and guidelines:

File Requirements:
• Video file size must not exceed 25 MB for optimal streaming performance
• Include a high-quality thumbnail image (recommended 1280x720 pixels)
• Supported formats For Videos: MP4
• Supported formats For Thumbnails: JPG

Optional Customization:
• You may optionally add your YouTube channel link or credits at the beginning of your video
• This helps viewers discover your original content

How to Submit:
Click the "Upload" button below to send an email to MintyFlinty, the founder and CEO of LimeShelf. Include your video file and thumbnail in your submission. Our team will review your content and add it to our platform within everyday at 4:30 PM EST.

Thank you for contributing to LimeShelf!
